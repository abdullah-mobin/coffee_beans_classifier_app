Origin of the Coffee Bean Roast Classifier



The Coffee Bean Roast Classifier was established in 2024 as a powerful tool to help identify and classify coffee beans based on their roast level. With the growing demand for coffee quality assessment, this classifier uses deep learning models to analyze the appearance of coffee beans and determine their roast grade with high accuracy.
The project began with the goal of providing a practical solution for coffee roasters, suppliers, and enthusiasts to evaluate the roast level of beans, assisting in product quality control and consistency.
